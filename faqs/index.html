Frequently Asked Questions

01

What is Ruqtec?

Ruqtec is an EdTech computer dedicated to providing accessible and high-quality tech education at a very affordable rate. We offer a range of programs in web development, both frontend and backend, data science, product design and others. Our programs are aimed at empowering individuals to excel in the tech industry.

02

Are Ruqtec's programs suitable for beginners?

Absolutely. We offer programs for learners of all levels, from beginners to advanced. Our courses are designed to cater to a wide range of tech enthusiasts, ensuring everyone can benefit from our education.

03

Can I spread my payments?

Yes, you can spread your payment via our Installment payment option, just email the finance team via finance@ruqtec.com if you have any questions about finances.

04

How does Ruqtec's learning platform work?

All sessions are held online. You can enroll for our courses, access course materials, complete assignments, and interact with instructors and fellow learners entirely online from anywhere in the world.

05

Are live sessions recorded?

Yes, all classes are held online and they will also be recorded. Recordings are available to all learners after each sessions. Learners are advised to attend as much live sessions as possible as this will be part of graduation criteria.

06

Can I combine this with my jobs/studies

Yes, you can combine our programs together with your jobs/studies as we will only require few hours of your 24hrs. Also you can watch the recorded lives sessions which will be made available to everyone after the class.

07

Is there a community aspect to Ruqtec?

Yes, we believe in the strength of community. Ruqtec fosters a supportive and collaborative environment where learners can connect, network, and grow together. Our community encourages knowledge sharing, idea exchange, and shared experiences.
